Quick Friday post, mostly to give you this: [learnelm.dev](https://learnelm.dev).

It's a curated link directory. Nothing fancy. Just the stuff I'd actually send a friend if they asked me where to start with Elm -- the official guide, the blog posts I keep linking to, talks that are worth an hour of your time, the playgrounds, the books.

If something's missing -- and something is _definitely_ missing, as I haven't spent a lot of time on this -- PRs are more than welcome. I'd much rather have your favorite Elm resource on there than not.

Have a great weekend :)
